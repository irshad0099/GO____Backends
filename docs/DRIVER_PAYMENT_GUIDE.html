Cash Payment
Passenger physically driver ko cash deta hai — sabse common method
Kya hota hai
Passenger ride complete hone ke baad driver ko cash physically deta hai. Driver ke haath mein poora fare aata hai — jisme company ka hissa bhi hota hai.
Kab hota hai
Jab passenger ne ride book karte waqt payment method = cash select kiya ho. Ride request mein ye dikha dega.
Kyun important hai
Driver ke paas company ka paisa bhi hota hai. Woh platform fee deposit na kare toh naye rides milne band ho jaate hain.
💡 Paise ka hisaab — example ₹120 ride
Passenger ka total fare ₹120.00
Platform fee (company ka hissa ~15%) - ₹18.00
Driver ki net earning = ₹102.00
Driver ke haath mein physically ₹120 (dono ka paise)
⚠️ Wallet mein immediately paisa NAHI aata. Driver ko pehle ₹18 company ko deposit karna hoga — tab ₹102 wallet mein credit hoga. Ye Rapido/Ola wala model hai — driver fraud rokne ke liye.
Step-by-Step Flow — Driver Kya Kare
1
Ride accept karo

Socket event ride:new_request mein paymentMethod: "cash" dikhega. Driver ko pata chal jaye ki ye cash ride hai.

2
Ride complete karo — drop pe pahuncho

PATCH status=completed call karo. Abhi payment nahi hui.

3
Passenger se cash lo — ₹120

Physically cash lo. Change do agar chahiye. Abhi screen pe pending dues dikhega — socket event driver:cash_dues_updated aayega.

4
Collect-Confirm API call karo

"Maine cash le liya" — ye confirm karna zaroori hai. Iske baad backend dues track karta hai.

5
Platform fee deposit karo (₹18)

Driver ke paas ₹120 hai — usmein ₹18 company ka hai. Ye deposit karna zaroori hai. Daily/weekly karo. Limit exceed hone se pehle karo.

6
₹102 wallet mein aa gaya ✅

Deposit submit karte hi backend held earnings (₹102) automatically wallet mein credit kar deta hai.

Personal UPI Payment
Passenger directly driver ke personal UPI ID pe pay karta hai
Kya hota hai
Passenger driver ka UPI QR scan karta hai ya UPI ID pe pay karta hai. Paise seedha driver ke bank account mein jaate hain.
Kab hota hai
Jab passenger ne personal_upi select kiya ho. Cash jaise hi hai — difference sirf transfer method ka hai.
Cash se farq
Physically cash nahi — digital transfer. Lekin company ka paisa driver ke paas hi rehta hai (bank mein). Deposit same tarike se karna hoga.

QR Code Payment
Driver GoMobility ka QR generate karta hai — passenger scan karke pay karta hai. No cash exchange, no deposit.
Kya hota hai
Driver app se GoMobility ka dynamic UPI QR generate hota hai. Passenger apne UPI app (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm) se scan karke pay karta hai — paise company ke UPI pe jaate hain.
Kab hota hai
Jab passenger ne ride book karte waqt qr payment method select kiya ho. Ya driver cash lene ke bajaye QR dikhana chahta ho.
Personal UPI se farq
Personal UPI = paise driver ke personal account mein jaate hain → deposit karna padta hai.

QR (GoMobility) = paise company ke account mein jaate hain → deposit nahi, earning auto-credit.
💡 Paise ka hisaab — ₹120 ride
Passenger QR scan karke pay karta hai₹120.00
Paise jaate haingomobility@upi (company)
Platform fee (company)- ₹18.00
Driver wallet mein credit= ₹102.00 ← automatic
✅ Driver ko deposit nahi karna. QR pe payment verify hote hi backend automatically driver ka ₹102 wallet mein credit kar deta hai. Wallet rides jaisa hi experience.
⏱️ QR 15 minutes mein expire ho jaata hai. Agar passenger 15 min mein scan na kare toh QR close karo aur naya generate karo ya dusra method use karo.
Step-by-Step Flow — Driver Kya Kare
1
Ride complete karo — PATCH status=completed

Pehle ride status complete karo. Phir QR generate karo.

2
QR Generate karo — API call karo

Backend se QR image milegi (base64 format). App mein screen pe dikhao — bada aur clear dikhna chahiye.

3
Passenger ko scan karne do

Passenger apna PhonePe / GPay / Paytm / koi bhi UPI app kholta hai → QR scan karta hai → amount auto-fill hota hai → pay karta hai.

4
Status poll karo ya verify API call karo

Passenger ke pay karne ke baad verify API call karo confirm karne ke liye. Ya 3-5 sec interval pe status poll karo.

5
Payment confirmed → QR close karo → Earning wallet mein ✅

Verify success hone pe QR close karo. Driver ka ₹102 automatically wallet mein credit.

6
QR expire ho gaya? → Close karo → Dusra try karo

Agar 15 min mein scan na hua toh QR close API call karo. Passenger ko cash ya dusra method use karne kaho.

POST /api/v1/payments/qr/close
QR code close/expire karo — payment complete ya cancel hone pe
📌 Kab: (1) Payment verify hone ke baad — used QR ko close karo. (2) Passenger ne scan nahi kiya, driver dusra method use karna chahta hai. (3) 15 min expire hone pe.
Request — Body
{
  "order_number": "ORD-1716123456-AB3K"
}
Response — Success (200)
{
  "success": true,
  "message": "QR payment closed successfully",
  "data": {}
}
💡 Polling Logic — Frontend mein kaise implement karo
// QR generate hone ke baad ye logic chalao:

const orderNumber = "ORD-1716123456-AB3K";
const maxWait = 15 * 60 * 1000; // 15 minutes
const startTime = Date.now();

const pollInterval = setInterval(async () => {
  // 15 min ho gaye? Polling band karo
  if (Date.now() - startTime > maxWait) {
    clearInterval(pollInterval);
    // QR close karo, user ko batao
    await closeQR(orderNumber);
    showMessage("QR expire ho gaya. Dusra method try karo.");
    return;
  }

  const res = await fetch(`/api/v1/payments/qr/status/${orderNumber}`);
  const data = await res.json();

  if (data.data.qrStatus === "completed") {
    clearInterval(pollInterval);
    await closeQR(orderNumber);   // QR close karo
    showSuccess("Payment complete! ₹102 wallet mein aa gaya.");
  }
}, 4000); // Har 4 seconds mein check
📷 QR Screen Design Tips: QR image ko kam se kam 280×280px dikhao. Saath mein amount bhi bada dikhao (₹120). Timer countdown dikhao (15:00 se 00:00). Agar expire ho jaaye toh "QR Refresh karo" button dikhao.

⚡ Payment Related Socket Events — Driver Kya Sunne
Event Name Kab Aata Hai Data
driver:cash_dues_updated Cash/UPI ride complete ke baad collect-confirm pe netEarnings, platformShareDue, message
driver:earnings_credited Wallet / Card / Corporate / QR ride ka earning credit hone pe netEarnings, walletBalance, paymentMethod
ride:status_changed QR verify hone ke baad ride status update confirm rideId, status, timestamp
⚠️ Important Rules — Frontend Developer Note
🚫 Cash/UPI rides mein collect-confirm se pehle status=completed mat karo — ride complete, phir payment lo, phir confirm karo. Sahi order: status=completed → cash lo → collect-confirm
💰 Cash limit exceed hone pe driver ko block karo — isLimitExceeded: true aane pe ride accept button disable karo aur deposit karne ka prominent prompt dikhao.
📱 Wallet balance update — Har earning credit hone ke baad socket event se balance update karo. REST API baar baar mat call karo — socket se real-time update lo.
✅ Idempotency — Agar collect-confirm ya cash/deposit same ride ke liye dobara call ho jaaye, backend safely handle karta hai — duplicate charge nahi hoga.
📷 QR Rules: (1) QR generate hone ke baad orderNumber zaroor save karo — verify aur close dono mein chahiye. (2) Payment hone ke baad QR close karna bhoolna mat — warna dusra koi use kar sakta hai. (3) 15 min ka timer screen pe dikhao — user ko pata ho kitna time bacha hai. (4) QR expire ho gaye toh qr/close call karo phir naya generate karo.
