1. Payment System Overview

GoMobility mein 5 tarah ke payment methods hain. Har method ka alag flow hai. Passenger choose karta hai method ride book karte waqt. Driver ko ussi ke hisaab se kaam karna hota hai.

Payment Method Passenger Kya Karta Hai Driver Kya Karta Hai Fraud Risk
wallet App wallet se auto-pay Kuch nahi — auto credit ✅ Zero
cash Physical cash deta hai Cash leke confirm karta hai ⚠️ High (fixed)
personal_upi Driver ki UPI pe scan karta hai UPI receive keke confirm karta hai ⚠️ High (fixed)
online / card Razorpay QR / card se pay Kuch nahi — webhook pe auto credit ✅ Zero
corporate Company invoice hoti hai Kuch nahi — platform credit karta hai ✅ Zero

Fare Calculation — Driver Ko Kitna Milta Hai

Passenger ne pay kiya: ₹120 (finalFare) Platform commission (18%): -₹18 (platformFee) ──────────────────────────────────── Driver net earnings: ₹102 ← wallet mein aata hai + Extras (agar applicable): Pickup compensation: +₹5 (door se pickup tha) Waiting charges: +₹10 (driver wait kiya) Traffic compensation: +₹3 (jam mein tha)

2. Kya Galat Tha — Bugs Found & Fixed

⚠️
4 critical bugs mile the original code mein. Sab fix ho gaye hain. Neeche detail mein explain kiya hai kya galat tha aur kya sahi kiya.

Bug 1 — CRITICAL: Wallet Ride Pe Driver Wallet Credit NAHI Hota Tha

❌ Pehle — Galat

Wallet ride complete hoti thi — passenger ka paisa wallet se kata jaata tha, lekin driver ke wallet mein kuch nahi jaata tha.

rideService.js → payForRide(passenger)   ✅ passenger debit hua
rideService.js → creditDriverEarnings()  ❌ YEH CALL THHHI HI NAHI
rideCompletionWorker → total_rides +1    ✅ sirf stats
Driver wallet balance = unchanged        ❌ PAISA GAYA KAHAN?
✅ Ab — Sahi

rideService.js mein wallet payment ke turant baad creditDriverEarnings() call ki jaati hai.

rideService.js → payForRide(passenger)      ✅ passenger debit
rideService.js → creditDriverEarnings()     ✅ driver credit turant
  (earningsService → wallet balance += ₹102)
  (cash dues agar hain → auto-deduct bhi)

Bug 2 — CRITICAL: total_earnings Double Count Hota Tha

❌ Pehle — Galat

Online ride pe total_earnings column 2 baar update hota tha.

earningsService.creditDriverEarnings():
  drivers.total_earnings = 0 + 87 = ₹87   ← pehli baar

rideCompletionWorker:
  drivers.total_earnings = 87 + 87 = ₹174  ← doosri baar ❌

Result: Dashboard pe ₹174 dikhta tha jabki actual ₹87 tha
✅ Ab — Sahi

rideCompletionWorker se total_earnings update hataaya. Ab sirf earningsService update karta hai — jab paisa actually wallet mein aata hai.

rideCompletionWorker ab sirf:
  drivers.total_rides = 10 + 1 = 11   ✅ rides count
  coupon record karo (agar tha)        ✅

total_earnings → earningsService update karta hai ✅ (ek baar)

Bug 3 — Cash Ride Pe Driver Wallet Credit Turant Ho Jaata Tha (Fraud Risk)

❌ Pehle — Galat (Fraud Possible)
Passenger cash deta hai driver ko: ₹120

Driver confirm karta hai → backend:
  Driver wallet credit: +₹102   ❌ TURANT
  Cash dues track:       ₹18    (sirf record, koi enforcement nahi)

Driver ke paas:
  Physical cash: ₹120
  Wallet:        ₹102
  Total:         ₹222  ← ₹18 kabhi na de — system rok nahi sakta tha
✅ Ab — Rapido Model
Passenger cash deta hai driver ko: ₹120

Driver confirm karta hai → backend:
  pending_amount       += ₹18   (company ka — held)
  pending_net_earnings += ₹102  (driver ka — bhi held)
  Wallet credit:         ₹0     ✅ kuch nahi gaya

Paisa milega jab:
  Option A: Driver ₹18 deposit kare → ₹102 wallet mein
  Option B: Agle online ride pe ₹18 auto-deduct → ₹102 release

Bug 4 — Minor: Duplicate Function + Dead Import

❌ Pehle — Confusing
creditDriverEarnings() do jagah define thi:
  walletService.js    ← purani simple version (no idempotency)
  earningsService.js  ← naya full version (idempotency + cash dues)

rideCollectionService.js mein import tha par call nahi hoti thi (dead code)
✅ Ab — Clean
creditDriverEarnings() sirf ek jagah:
  earningsService.js  ✅ (idempotency + cash auto-deduction)

walletService.js se purani function delete
rideCollectionService.js se dead import delete

3. Payment Method 1 — Wallet

👛
Wallet Payment
Passenger ke app wallet se auto-debit. Sabse simple aur safest.
Step 1 — Ride Book Karo
Passenger ride request karta hai
POST /api/v1/rides/request → payment_method: "wallet"
Step 2 — Driver Accept Karta Hai
Socket + REST API dono se accept hota hai
POST /api/v1/rides/:id/accept
Step 3 — Ride Complete
Driver status completed mark karta hai
PATCH /api/v1/rides/:id/status → { status: "completed" }
Step 4 — Passenger Wallet Debit (Auto)
Backend turant passenger wallet se ₹120 kaata hai
payForRide() → wallets.balance -= ₹120 (idempotent)
Step 5 — Driver Wallet Credit (Auto)
Passenger debit ke baad driver ko ₹102 milta hai
creditDriverEarnings() → wallets.balance += ₹102 + cash dues auto-deduct agar pending tha
Step 6 — Stats Update (Queue)
BullMQ queue mein job jaata hai
rideCompletionWorker → total_rides += 1, coupon record

Idempotency Protection

payForRide() → ride_id se existing debit check → agar hai toh skip ✅ creditDriverEarnings() → driver_earnings_transactions check → agar hai toh skip ✅

4. Payment Method 2 — Cash (Rapido Model)

💵
Cash Payment
Passenger physically cash deta hai driver ko. Fraud prevention ke liye Rapido model implement kiya hai.
💡
Rapido Model: Driver ka paisa wallet mein TURANT nahi aata. Pehle platform fee settle karni hoti hai — tabhi earnings release hoti hain.
Step 1 — Ride Complete
Status complete, koi wallet transaction nahi hoti
rides.payment_status = 'pending' (kuch nahi kata/credit)
Step 2 — Passenger Cash Deta Hai (Physical)
Driver ke haath mein ₹120 cash aata hai
No API — physical transaction
Step 3 — Driver Cash Confirm Karta Hai
Driver app mein "Cash Mila" tap karta hai
POST /api/v1/cash-payment/confirm → pending_amount += ₹18 (company ka — held) → pending_net_earnings += ₹102 (driver ka — held) → Wallet: ₹0 change (kuch nahi mila abhi)
Step 4A — Driver Platform Fee Deposit Karta Hai (Manual)
Driver UPI/bank se company ko ₹18 bhejta hai
POST /api/v1/drivers/cash/deposit → pending_amount -= ₹18 → releaseNetEarnings() → ₹102 wallet mein credit ✅ → total_earnings += ₹102 ✅
Step 4B — Auto Deduction (Next Online Ride Se)
Driver ne manual deposit nahi kiya — agle online ride pe auto-deduct
creditDriverEarnings() — online ride pe: Online earning: ₹95 Cash dues auto-deduct: -₹18 Net wallet credit: ₹77 + Held ₹102 bhi release → wallet mein ✅

5. Payment Method 3 — Personal UPI

📱
Personal UPI
Passenger driver ki personal UPI pe scan karta hai. Cash jaisa hi — same Rapido model.

Personal UPI aur Cash ka backend flow bilkul same hai. Fark sirf itna hai ki passenger physically cash nahi deta — UPI app se driver ke number pe transfer karta hai.

Passenger → Driver UPI pe ₹120 bhejta hai (Google Pay / PhonePe) Driver confirm karta hai → POST /cash-payment/confirm { method: "personal_upi" } Baaki sab same as Cash: pending_amount += ₹18 (company ka) pending_net_earnings += ₹102 (driver ka — held) Release: deposit ya auto-deduction pe
ℹ️
API endpoint aur payload Cash ke bilkul same hai — sirf "method": "personal_upi" use karo.

6. Payment Method 4 — Online / Card (Razorpay)

💳
Online / Card
Razorpay QR ya Card se payment. Platform ke paas paisa aata hai — driver ko automatically credit.
Step 1 — Ride Complete
payment_status = 'pending' (Razorpay ka wait)
PATCH /api/v1/rides/:id/status → { status: "completed" }
Step 2 — Passenger Razorpay QR Scan Karta Hai
Passenger app mein QR dikhta hai, scan karo
Razorpay payment gateway handle karta hai
Step 3 — Razorpay Webhook Aata Hai
Payment confirm hone pe Razorpay backend ko notify karta hai
POST /api/v1/payments/webhook (Razorpay se)
Step 4 — postPaymentWorker Process Karta Hai
Ride payment_status update + driver earnings
processOnlinePaymentSuccess(): rides.payment_status = 'paid' creditDriverEarnings() → driver wallet += ₹102 cash dues auto-deduct agar pending tha ✅
🔒
Idempotency: Agar webhook 2 baar aaye, payment_status === 'paid' check se second call skip ho jaati hai.

7. Payment Method 5 — Corporate

🏢
Corporate
Company ki taraf se billing. Driver ko immediately credit — platform collection risk leta hai.
Ride complete hoti hai ↓ processCorporateBilling(): rides.payment_status = 'billed_corporate' creditDriverEarnings(paymentMethod: 'corporate') → Driver wallet += netEarnings ✅ (turant) ↓ Company ko monthly invoice jaati hai (backend admin handle karta hai)
ℹ️
Corporate rides mein driver cash confirm API call nahi karta. Agar kare toh error milega: "Corporate rides are billed directly to the company"

10. Fraud Prevention — Cash Model Detail

Problem Statement

Pehle ka system: Driver ₹120 cash physically leta tha + ₹102 wallet mein bhi milta tha. ₹18 company ka — koi enforcement nahi tha. Driver kabhi deposit na kare — system rok nahi sakta tha.

3 Layer Protection (Rapido Jaisa)

Layer 1 — No Upfront Wallet Credit

Cash confirm karo:
  wallet: ₹0 change  ← fraud impossible
  pending_net_earnings: ₹102 held ← driver ka paisa ruka hai
  pending_amount: ₹18 tracked ← company ka paisa track hua

Layer 2 — Auto Deduction from Online Earnings

Driver ne agle din online ride ki. Earning: ₹95.

creditDriverEarnings() automatically:
  Cash dues check: pending_amount = ₹18
  Auto-deduct: ₹18
  Net credit: ₹77 (95-18)
  + Held ₹102 bhi wallet mein release ✅

Driver chahe deposit na kare — system khud kat lega.

Layer 3 — Cash Limit Block

pending_amount >= ₹2000 (cash_limit):
  is_limit_exceeded = TRUE
  Driver ko naya ride accept karne se rok sakte hain (admin action)

2 ghante baad bhi pending:
  payment_status = 'admin_review_required'
  Admin ko alert
