2. Eligibility & Onboarding

4. KYC & Document Compliance

5. Ride Acceptance & Conduct

5.1 Accepting Rides

  • You may accept or reject ride requests at your discretion.
  • Consistently high rejection rates may affect your standing on the platform.
  • Once a ride is accepted, you must proceed to the pickup location promptly.

5.2 OTP Verification

  • Ask the Passenger for the OTP to start the ride. Do not start the ride without OTP verification.
  • Marking a ride as started without OTP is a violation and may result in penalties.

5.3 Ride Completion

  • Complete the ride only at the agreed drop-off location.
  • Mark the ride as completed in the app — this triggers payment and earnings credit.

5.4 Driver Conduct

  • Treat all Passengers with respect and professionalism.
  • Do not cancel rides excessively or manipulate the platform.
  • Do not accept rides outside the platform or demand cash separately from the app fare.
  • Do not engage in discrimination based on gender, religion, caste, or destination.
