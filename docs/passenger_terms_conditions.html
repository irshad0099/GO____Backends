4. Booking & Ride Process

4.1 Requesting a Ride

  • Enter your pickup and drop-off location in the app.
  • Select a vehicle type (Bike / Auto / Car).
  • A fare estimate is shown before you confirm the booking.
  • Once a Driver accepts, you will receive Driver details and an ETA.

4.2 OTP Verification

  • A one-time OTP is shared with you when the Driver arrives at pickup.
  • Share the OTP with the Driver to begin the ride. Do not share OTP before the Driver arrives.

4.3 Ride Completion

  • The ride ends when the Driver marks it as completed at the drop-off location.
  • An invoice is generated automatically and available in the app.
